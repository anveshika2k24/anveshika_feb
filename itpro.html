IT PROFESSIONAL • ARAVIND KRISHNA

Aravind Krishna: Shaping the Future of Enterprise Technology

Aravind Krishna is both the CEO and Chairman of IBM. He has served as CEO since April 2020 and became Chairman in January 2021.

Born in Andhra Pradesh, India, he studied electrical engineering at IIT Kanpur and later completed his PhD at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign.

He believes technology should be useful, trustworthy, and beneficial to society, promoting responsible AI and continuous learning.

IT PROFESSIONAL • SATYA NADELLA

Satya Nadella: Leading with Empathy and Innovation

Satya Nadella is Chairman and CEO of Microsoft. He transformed the company through cloud computing and AI innovation.

He joined Microsoft in 1992 and played a key role in developing Azure. In 2014, he became CEO and introduced a growth mindset culture.

Known for empathy and humility, he believes technology should empower people and be used responsibly.
