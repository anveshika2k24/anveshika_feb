Genpact: Transforming Businesses Through Innovation and Digital Excellence

Genpact is a global professional services and technology company that helps businesses improve operations using data, analytics, automation, and artificial intelligence. Founded in 1997 in Gurgaon as a unit of GE, it became independent in 2005 and is headquartered in New York City.

The company is led by Balkrishan “BK” Kalra (CEO since 2024) and employs over 140,000 professionals worldwide. Genpact serves industries like banking, healthcare, insurance, and manufacturing, delivering digital transformation through advanced tools such as its AI platform, Genpact Cora.

The company also offers strong career opportunities, internships, and campus recruitment programs for students and fresh graduates.

Tata Consultancy Services (TCS): A Global Leader in IT Services and Consulting

Tata Consultancy Services (TCS) is one of the world’s largest IT services, consulting, and business solutions companies and a part of the respected Tata Group. Founded in 1968 and headquartered in Mumbai, TCS played a pioneering role in developing India’s IT industry.

The company is led by K. Krithivasan and focuses on innovation, digital transformation, and sustainable growth. With over 600,000 employees across more than 50 countries, TCS serves clients worldwide in software development, cloud computing, artificial intelligence, data analytics, and cybersecurity.

TCS offers strong career and internship opportunities and is committed to corporate social responsibility through initiatives in education, healthcare, digital literacy, and environmental sustainability.
